    Futile

    (Few-dil) ADJECTIVE- Something completely useless and redundant, unable to provide any feasable use that results in a particular outcome.

    EX:"We don't have enough time, trying to buy some would be futile ."

    Synonyms Antonyms
    Pointless Vital
    Usless Important
    Unnessesary Crucial

    Bigotry

    (Big-o-tree) NOUN- A belief to which one is attatched or commited to, often being biased and prejudiced against a certain group(such as racism, ect).

    EX:"What do you mean I can't eat here? Is it because I have a samsung?This is an act of bigotry! Bigotry, I say!"

    Synonyms Antonyms
    Bias Inclusive
    Prejudice Tolerance
    Injustice Fairness

    Pompous

    (Pom-pus) ADJECTIVE- To be extremely self-centered, self-important and concieted, to the point where it become irritating to others.

    EX:"Linda did you hear about Charles? He's such a pompous fool who thinks he's a master at everything, he gets me so mad!"

    Synonyms Antonyms
    Show-off Humble
    Egotistical selfless
    Arrogant Modest

    Lovelorn

    (Luv-lorn) ADJECTIVE- To be extremely sad and disheartened due to the deprivation of love and affection returning to them.

    EX:"Lovelorn Haily was caught in an imbalanced relationship, as her partner did not show adequate affection."

    Synonyms Antonyms
    Love-deprived Loved
