Sonder

(Son-der) NOUN- The feeling of acknowledging the reality that everyone else around you has a life just as complex as yours.

EX:"Suddenly Jimmy had the sonder thought, that Maddie might be going through a tragedy so horrid such as his."

Synonyms Antonyms
Awareness Apathy
Understanding Insensitivity

Tomfoolery

(Tom-foo-ler-ee) NOUN- A foolish or mischeivous, often comical act, notion, behavior, or situation.

EX:" Grandpa burst open the door and shouted "Boy, what is this tomfoolery I hear goin' on!"

Synonyms Antonyms
Foolishness Maturity
Missbehavior Formality
