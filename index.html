Sonder

(Son-der) NOUN- The feeling of acknowledging the reality that everyone else around you has a life just as complex as yours.

EX:"Suddenly Jimmy had the sonder thought, that Maddie might be going through a tragedy so horrid such as his."

Synonyms Antonyms
Awareness Apathy
