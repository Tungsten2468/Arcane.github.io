    Sonder

    (Son-der) NOUN- The feeling of acknowledging the reality that everyone else around you has a life just as complex as yours.

    EX:"Suddenly Jimmy had the sonder thought, that Maddie might be going through a tragedy so horrid, such as his own."

    Synonyms Antonyms
    Awareness Apathy
    Understanding Insensitivity

    Tomfoolery

    (Tom-foo-ler-ee) NOUN- A foolish or mischeivous, often comical act, notion, behavior, or situation.

    EX:"Grandpa burst open the door and shouted "Boy, what is this tomfoolery I hear goin' on!"

    Synonyms Antonyms
    Foolishness Maturity
    Missbehavior Formality

    Lugubrious

    (Lu-goo-bree-ous) ADJECTIVE- Of a thing: to be extremely sad and miserable, to the point where it seems almost exaggerated or even maybe funnily overwhelming.

    EX:"They found Sarah alone on the curb, the lugubrious expression on her face as she eyed her fallen icecream."

    Synonyms Antonyms
    Sad Chipper
    Dismal Happy
    Melancholic Joyful
