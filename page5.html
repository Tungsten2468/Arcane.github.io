    Transmute

    (Trans-mee-oot) VERB- In alchemy: the practice of changing the nature of a substance or matter into a completely different nature or matter.

    EX:"Our modern technology may allow us to transmute a mineral into a much stronger one."

    Synonyms Antonyms
    Transform Destroy

    Tipsy

    (Tip-see) ADJECTIVE- Describing a person who is slightly drunk, but not entirely under the influence.

    EX:"One shot of beer got her sort of tipsy."

    Denouement

    (Day-noo-mah-n) NOUN- The concluding part of a something, especially if refering to entertaining works such as plays, shows, performances, ect.

    EX:"That denouement was wonderful!"
