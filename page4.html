    Malinger

    (Ma-ling-ger) VERB- The act of feigning illness or pretending to be sick, often in order to be excused from an unpleasant situation or job.

    EX:"Chris's plan to malinger so that he wouldn't have to attend school ended up in failiure."

    Synonyms Antonyms
    Act Confess
    Trick Concede
    Feign Admit
